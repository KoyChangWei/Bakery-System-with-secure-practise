Our Story

Since the 1980s, Roti Sri has been a cornerstone of the Alor Star community, serving fresh, quality baked goods that have brought smiles to generations of families. Under the dedicated leadership of Encik Razak Hassan, we've maintained our commitment to traditional baking excellence while embracing modern innovations to better serve our customers.

Today, we're excited to embark on a new chapter in our story. We're modernizing our operations to enhance your experience while maintaining the same quality and care that you've come to expect from Roti Sri. Our new digital systems will allow us to serve you better, whether you're visiting our store or ordering online.

Our commitment to the community remains stronger than ever. We're not just a bakery; we're your neighbors, your friends, and your go-to destination for fresh, delicious baked goods. As we embrace these changes, we promise to maintain the warm, personal touch that has made Roti Sri a beloved name in Alor Star.

Thank you for being part of our journey. We look forward to continuing to serve you and your family for generations to come.
